About Europe Gateway

These popular destinations have a lot to offer, Europe Gateway CO. We are a local DMC based in Cairo, Egypt. Established in 2012 by experienced travel industry leaders, and a strong team each with over 13 years of experience in the field.

We have service branches in Egypt’s major touristic cities, Luxor, Aswan, Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh we can guarantee the best possible client experience all over the country.

We cover all aspects of travel services E.G. from a simple hotel reservation to a mega Conference Organization or an Event planning; Launching a Product Ceremony on a Nile Cruise, a special Board meeting on a yacht cruising the Mediterranean or Red Sea, A Theme party in the desert with Bedouin costume etc. We serve Corporate Travel, Leisure Packages, Conferences and Events Management, Haj and Umra trips, Inbound & Outbound Tours, Religious Islamic & Christian trips, in addition to Sports travel (Golf, Sea Sports, Surfing, Diving, etc..) with more than 70 employees, we support our customers with ultimate quality & dedicated services 24/7.

We have furthered our mission, ensuring that your travels leave a positive imprint on the planet. Each trip with Europe Gateway CO.is a step towards a more sustainable and respectful global community.

Europe Gateway Team

Our dedicated team is currently made up of 70 staff including Tour Operators, Managers, aviation manager, drivers, bookings and reservations team. Each with relevant industry and training qualifications for their roles.

The number of our team members constantly increases as we grow year after year.

Europe Gateway CO. believe that people of all genders have equal rights, responsibilities and opportunities. Almost half of our staff are highly educated talented females.

Our Vision & Mission

What drives us at Europe Gateway

Our Vision

We envision a world where travel is more than just a destination — it’s a transformative journey. At Europe Gateway, we aim to redefine exploration by creating experiences that inspire curiosity, build cultural bridges, and leave lasting impressions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver personalized, high-quality travel experiences that exceed expectations. With a blend of innovation and local expertise, we craft seamless journeys that reflect our clients’ values — built on trust, cultural respect, and a commitment to sustainability.
