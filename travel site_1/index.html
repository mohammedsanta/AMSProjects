About Europe Gateway

These popular destinations have a lot to offer

Europe Gateway CO. We are a local DMC based in Cairo, Egypt. Established in 2012 by experienced travel industry leaders, and a strong team each with over 13 years of experience in the field.

We have service branches in Egypt’s major touristic cities, Luxor, Aswan, Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh we can guarantee the best possible client experience all over the country.

We serve Corporate Travel, Leisure Packages, Conferences and Events Management, Haj and Umra trips, Inbound & Outbound Tours, Religious Islamic & Christian trips, in addition to Sports travel (Golf, Sea Sports, Surfing, Diving, etc..) with more than 70 employees, we support our customers with ultimate quality & dedicated services 24/7.
